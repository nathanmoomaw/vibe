# ETHOS — vibe

Sound you live inside, not sound you listen to.

No tracks. No progress bars. No play/pause anxiety. VIBE hums at the frequency of wherever you are and whatever you need. You dial in the color. It does the rest.

Colored noise is real science. Brown noise calms dogs and children. Pink noise sharpens focus. White noise erases the world. VIBE knows this and never lets the bad harmonics through.

The ambient evocations — bell, wind, water, earth — are not recordings. They're synthesized from scratch, approaching the essence of these sounds without pretending to be them.

This is a frequency experience. Not a product.
