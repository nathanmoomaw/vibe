# MYTHOS — vibe

## The Six States

**calming · relaxing · focussing · meditating · dreaming · floating**

These are what VIBE is for. Not ambient music. Not noise generation. *State induction.*

Each state has a character:
- **calming** — tension releasing, like a warm room after cold air
- **relaxing** — body unwinding, breath slowing, the next thought arriving later
- **focussing** — a narrow cone of attention, background dissolved
- **meditating** — presence without content, awareness without object
- **dreaming** — the threshold state, hypnagogic, loosely associative
- **floating** — weightless, unmoored from time, the body forgotten

VIBE makes noise to disappear into.

The lineage goes: ribbon (play) → puddle (listen) → vibe (become).

The vintage white noise machines it draws from were practical objects — sold for sleep, for privacy, for baby rooms. But anyone who's ever stared at one of those little brown boxes knows they are also portals. You dial one up, you go somewhere.

VIBE is that, with color. With pulse. With aura.

The astro/divination layer isn't decoration — it's the same instinct. People have always used symbols and rituals to reach a certain frequency. VIBE just makes it visible.

Audness powers the sound. The soul comes from the lineage.
